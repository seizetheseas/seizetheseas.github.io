Our Mission

When our team of four were brainstorming ideas for our project, we knew we wanted to create a game that would inspire people to take philanthropic action within and outside of their local communities. We decided to focus on the topic of environmental justice, particularly relating to oceans around the world. Cleaning up the Earthâ€™s oceans is an issue we can all agree on.

According to the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO), over 220 million tons of plastic are produced each year. And it is a heartbreaking sight to see images of marine animals injured and dying from the waste we produce. Our game, Seize the Seas, aims to educate the public about the severe impact our simple acts can collectively have on the world around us.

-Seize the Seas Team
